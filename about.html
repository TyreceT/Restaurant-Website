About Cosmic Diner

History

It started with five friends, zero investors, and a wild idea—to build a 1950s-inspired diner that wasn’t just a place to eat, but a whole experience.

Tired of overpriced cafés and soulless fast food, they wanted something different:

  • 📸 Aesthetic enough for the ‘gram
  • 🍔 Affordable enough for students
  • 🎶 And vibey enough to feel like a movie scene

Armed with DIY skills, second-hand furniture, and a lot of YouTube tutorials, they built Cosmic Diner from scratch—painting walls, hunting down vintage décor, and stringing up neon signs by hand. The goal? To make every corner Instagram-worthy while serving up the best damn burgers and milkshakes in town.

But what really makes Cosmic Diner special isn’t just the neon lights or retro vibes—it’s the fact that the founders never left. They’re not just the brains behind the brand; they’re also the hands behind the grill, the voices that greet every customer, and the ones making sure every burger, shake, and plate of fries is served with heart.

Now, Cosmic Diner is the go-to spot for students, blending retro nostalgia with 2025 energy—and the best part? The same five friends who built it are still working behind the counter, flipping burgers, refilling shakes, and keeping the dream alive, one meal at a time.

Mission

Cosmic Diner is a student-built, student-run retro haven that blends 1950s nostalgia with 2025 energy. Our mission is to serve affordable, high-quality comfort food in a space that is as Instagrammable as it is welcoming. Every visit is more than just a meal—it’s an experience filled with neon lights, jukebox tunes, and good company.


Principles

🔥 DIY or Die. No investors, just passion, hard work, and student hustle.

👨‍🍳 Bosses Work the Grill. Our founders don’t sit in an office; they flip the burgers.

📸 If You Can’t Snap It, Did It Even Happen? Every inch of Cosmic is made for the ‘gram.

🍟 Affordable, Because We Get It. We’re students too, and we keep prices real.
